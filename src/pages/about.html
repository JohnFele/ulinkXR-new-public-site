Our Story

Founded in 2009, Ulink began its journey as a trusted provider of office automation solutions, specialising in printers, IT, CCTV and voice-over-IP services. For over a decade, we have built strong, lasting relationships with our customers across Gauteng and beyond, earning a reputation for reliability, service excellence and innovation. When the world changed after COVID, so did we. We pivoted our focus and poured our energy, expertise and passion into the future of education and business, bringing Virtual Reality, Robotics and Interactive Tech into classrooms and businesses across South Africa.
